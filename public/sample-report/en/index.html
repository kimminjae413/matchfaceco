4. Emission factor sources
  • Electricity — Vietnam : national grid emission factor published by the Department of Climate Change (Cục Biến đổi khí hậu), MONRE. Latest confirmed value 0.6592 tCO₂/MWh (for 2023, official letter 1726/BĐKH-PTCBT). The factor is updated annually; we apply the latest published value at the time of calculation.
  • Materials : material carbon factor database (PP · PE · ABS and others)
  • Logistics : emission factors by transport mode (sea · air · road)
  • Renewable share is deducted only against the plant's documented power procurement. Where no evidence exists, we conservatively treat it as 0%.
5. Standards and limitations
  • ISO 14067 — product-level carbon footprint quantification
  • GHG Protocol — relevance · completeness · consistency · transparency · accuracy
  • CBAM — reporting format for the EU carbon border mechanism
  • This is a lightweight PCF focused on manufacturing and logistics, not a full life-cycle assessment. Disclosures that must include the use and end-of-life stages require an extended boundary.
  • This document has not been verified by a third-party verification body. Where verification is required, a separate process applies.
